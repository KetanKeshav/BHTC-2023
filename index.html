Overview

Welcome to the 2nd edition of Bangalore Humanitarian Technology Conclave (BHTC) on the theme SDG-12: Responsible Production and Consumption.

The IEEE B-HTC 2022 is a flagship event of IEEE Bangalore Section. It is held annually as part of the Humanitarian outreach program of the Section; where technology leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, and thought provokers share their passion in addressing impactful problems, and vision on futuristic technologies that will/may disrupt/impact our way of working and living.

This edition of IEEE BHTC will be based on the theme of SDG-12 which is meant to ensure good use of resources, improving energy efficiency, sustainable infrastructure, and providing access to basic services, green and decent jobs and ensuring a better quality of life for all, with a specific focus on:

  1. Sustainable management and use of natural resources
  2. Responsible management of chemicals and food waste
  3. Substantially reduce waste generation
  4. Encourage companies to adopt sustainable practices and sustainability reporting
